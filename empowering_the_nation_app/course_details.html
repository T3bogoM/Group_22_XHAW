Landscaping

Duration: Six months

Purpose

This course equips learners with the knowledge and techniques needed to design, plan, and maintain outdoor spaces. Students learn about soil management, plant selection, and sustainable gardening practices that enhance both private and public environments.

Learning Outcomes

  • Identify and care for indigenous and exotic plants
  • Design balanced, visually appealing landscapes
  • Build and maintain fixed garden structures (fountains, benches, braais)
  • Apply water-efficient and eco-friendly gardening methods

Employment Opportunities

Landscaping companies, estate maintenance, garden design services, or independent contracting.
